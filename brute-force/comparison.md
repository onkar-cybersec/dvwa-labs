# Brute Force Comparison (DVWA)

## Low
- No protection
- Easy to brute force
- Clear response differences

## Medium
- Weak protection
- Slight delay
- Attack still successful

## High
- Stronger logic
- Responses appear similar
- Hard to detect correct password

## Conclusion
As security level increases, the application does not always block attacks, but reduces their effectiveness by hiding response differences and slowing automation.
